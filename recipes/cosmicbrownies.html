Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Melted chocolate
  • Baking powder
  • Raw Milk
  • Orb sprinkles

Steps

  1. Mix flour and milk
  2. Add baking powder
  3. Pour into brownie tray
  4. Bake in oven at 420 degrees fahrenheit
  5. Sprinkle sprinkles
  6. Enjoy!
