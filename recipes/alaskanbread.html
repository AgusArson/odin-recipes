Alaskan Bread Recipe

Description

Working with Alaskan sourdough bread gives a mild sweet taste, it is due to the simple ingredients needed to accomplish this meal. It is also a very versatile food that can be made from in your oven to deep in the cold woods under campfire coals. With a little perserverence and some heat anyone can make this bread right away and enjoy all the variety that comes with it.

Ingredients

  • Dough Flour
  • Yeast
  • Honey Butter
  • Raw Milk

Steps

  1. Mix Yeast with Dough Flour
  2. Let sit 4-6 hours
  3. Take out of bag and form into consumable food
  4. Wrap addons in now if you have them ie.gouda cheese, pulled pork
  5. bury in coals
  6. It's done when the coals fall off
  7. Enjoy!
