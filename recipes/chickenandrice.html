Ingredients

  • 2lbs Chicken Breast
  • Seasonings
  • Olive oil
  • Quality White Rice
  • Water

Steps

  1. Tenderize raw chicken
  2. Pour olive oil across chimken
  3. Add Seasonings
  4. Bake in oven at 420 degrees fahrenheit
  5. Cook rice in rice cooker
  6. Check chicken internal temp. for 165 degrees fahrenheit
  7. Cut into diagonal pieces
  8. Serve and Enjoy!
